Iolas Qintoras (Prologue)

In the calm village of Ellanora there was a young elf, around 17. Iolas Qintoris was his name, lived an ordinary life: Helping his parents around the house, going to the market every fortnight, yet worst of all, he got his first job. He sold old things around the market, and bimonthly, he got to dip arrows in different concoctions he made from moon lotus and a variety of other things, for hunting and selling for a large quantity of money. Most importantly, he loved his life. He never for a second took it for granted, he loved the luscious green trees and the moss that cushioned his feet on his hour long walk to the market in the city, and the flowers that he passed by every day. But one day... it all went wrong.
